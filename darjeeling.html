Trip Highlights

Tiger Hill Sunrise

Witness the magical sunrise over Mt. Kanchenjunga from Tiger Hill, one of the most spectacular sunrise viewpoints in the world at 8,482 feet altitude.

Tea Garden Experience

Explore the world-famous Darjeeling tea estates, learn about tea processing, and taste the finest teas while enjoying panoramic Himalayan views.

UNESCO Toy Train

Experience the historic Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, a UNESCO World Heritage site, on a scenic journey through mountains and tea gardens.

Tsomgo Lake

Visit the sacred glacial lake at 12,313 feet, surrounded by snow-capped mountains and considered holy by both Buddhists and Hindus.

Buddhist Monasteries

Explore ancient Buddhist monasteries including Rumtek, Pemayangtse, and Ghoom, experiencing peaceful spirituality and Tibetan culture.

Kanchenjunga Views

Marvel at the world's third highest peak Mt. Kanchenjunga (28,169 ft) from multiple viewpoints throughout your journey in Darjeeling and Sikkim.

Gangtok Exploration

Discover Sikkim's charming capital with its clean streets, vibrant markets, beautiful gardens, and stunning mountain backdrop.

Rich Cultural Heritage

Experience the unique blend of Nepali, Tibetan, and Bengali cultures through local festivals, cuisine, handicrafts, and warm hospitality.
