Trip Highlights

Rohtang Pass Adventure

Experience the thrill of high-altitude adventure at 13,050 feet with snow activities, stunning glacier views, and breathtaking mountain landscapes.

Spiritual Journey

Visit the residence of Dalai Lama in Dharamshala, explore ancient temples, and experience the peaceful Tibetan Buddhist culture in McLeod Ganj.

Adventure Sports

Enjoy paragliding in Solang Valley, trekking to Triund, river rafting, and various other adrenaline-pumping activities throughout the journey.

Colonial Heritage

Explore the rich colonial architecture of Shimla, Kasauli, and Dalhousie with their historic churches, Victorian buildings, and British-era charm.

Natural Beauty

Witness the pristine beauty of Khajjiar (Mini Switzerland), crystal clear lakes, dense forests, and panoramic mountain views throughout the trip.

UNESCO Toy Train

Experience the famous Kalka-Shimla railway, a UNESCO World Heritage site, with its scenic route through tunnels and beautiful mountain landscapes.

Natural Hot Springs

Relax and rejuvenate in the natural hot springs of Vashisht and Manikaran, known for their therapeutic properties and spiritual significance in Hindu mythology.

Local Markets & Culture

Explore vibrant local markets of Mall Road, shop for authentic Himachali handicrafts, woolen items, and experience the warm hospitality of mountain people.
