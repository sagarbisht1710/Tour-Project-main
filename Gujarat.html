Trip Highlights

Asiatic Lions Safari

Experience the thrill of spotting the majestic Asiatic Lions in their natural habitat at Gir National Park, the only place in the world where they exist in the wild.

UNESCO Heritage City

Explore Ahmedabad's walled city with its stunning Indo-Islamic architecture, ancient step-wells, and vibrant traditional neighborhoods called pols.

White Desert of Kutch

Witness the surreal beauty of Rann of Kutch, the world's largest salt desert, especially magical during full moon nights with its pristine white landscape.

Krishna's Sacred Dwarka

Visit Dwarka, one of the four sacred Hindu pilgrimage sites, believed to be the ancient kingdom of Lord Krishna with its magnificent temples and coastal beauty.
