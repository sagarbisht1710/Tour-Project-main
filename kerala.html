Trip Highlights

Backwater Houseboat Experience

Cruise through the serene backwaters of Alleppey on traditional houseboats, witnessing village life and enjoying freshly prepared Kerala cuisine while floating on tranquil waters.

Munnar Tea Plantations

Explore the rolling hills of Munnar covered with lush green tea gardens, visit tea museums, and learn about tea processing while enjoying breathtaking mountain views.

Pristine Kovalam Beaches

Relax on the golden sandy beaches of Kovalam, enjoy water sports, visit the iconic lighthouse, and experience rejuvenating Ayurvedic massages by the Arabian Sea.

Historic Fort Kochi

Discover the colonial charm of Fort Kochi with its Chinese fishing nets, ancient churches, spice markets, and the unique blend of Portuguese, Dutch, and British architecture.

Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary

Experience Kerala's rich biodiversity at Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary in Thekkady, enjoy boat rides on Periyar Lake, and spot elephants, tigers, and exotic birds in their natural habitat.

Spice Plantation Tours

Visit aromatic spice plantations in Thekkady, learn about organic farming of cardamom, pepper, cinnamon, and cloves, and take home authentic Kerala spices and herbs.

Cultural Performances

Experience Kerala's rich cultural heritage through traditional Kathakali dance performances, Kalaripayattu martial arts demonstrations, and local folk music shows.

Authentic Kerala Cuisine

Savor the flavors of Kerala with traditional dishes like fish curry, appam, puttu, and sadya served on banana leaves, along with fresh coconut water and tropical fruits.
