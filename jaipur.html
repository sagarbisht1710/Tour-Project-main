Trip Highlights

Amber Fort Majesty

Experience the grandeur of Rajput architecture with elephant rides, mirror work chambers, and breathtaking hilltop views of the surrounding landscape.

Royal City Palace

Explore the living heritage of Jaipur's royal family with stunning courtyards, museums, and architectural marvels that showcase centuries of royal history.

Iconic Hawa Mahal

Marvel at the Palace of Winds with its unique honeycomb structure, 953 windows, and pink sandstone facade that has become the symbol of Jaipur.
