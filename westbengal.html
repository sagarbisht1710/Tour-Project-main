Trip Highlights

Colonial Architecture

Explore magnificent colonial buildings including Victoria Memorial, Howrah Bridge, and St. Paul's Cathedral showcasing British-era architectural grandeur.

Tagore's Legacy

Visit Shantiniketan, the Nobel laureate's university town, and experience his vision of holistic education and cultural harmony in rural Bengal.

Royal Bengal Tigers

Experience thrilling boat safaris in Sundarbans mangrove forests searching for the majestic Royal Bengal Tigers in their natural habitat.

Terracotta Temples

Marvel at the intricate terracotta temples of Bishnupur with their detailed carvings depicting stories from Hindu epics and local folklore.
